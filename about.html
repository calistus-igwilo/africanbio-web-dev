About African Biosciences

Your Bioscience Research Partner In Africa

African Biosciences Ltd. is a pioneer and innovative life sciences company providing research reagents, supplies and technical support services to the academic, research, healthcare diagnostics and forensic testing sectors in Nigeria and across Africa (through its subsidiaries). We are dedicated to transforming life sciences research by providing scientific and technical support to the academic and research community by enabling and catalyzing the ability to conduct bioscience research.
