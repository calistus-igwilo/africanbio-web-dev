Welcome to African Biosiences

African Biosciences Ltd. is a pioneer and innovative life sciences company providing research reagents, supplies and technical support services to the academic, research, healthcare diagnostics and forensic testing sectors in Nigeria and across Africa. We are dedicated to transforming life sciences research by providing scientific and technical support to the academic and research community by enabling and catalyzing the ability to conduct bioscience research.

Our mission is to be the premier bioscience research service provider in Nigeria and across the African continent to improve bioscience research capacity by providing e-commerce access to reagents and supplies, research capabilities in genomics, proteomics, and bioinformatics, bioscience research advisory services, bioscience technical training, lab-for-rent, contract research services, and specialized medical testing and DNA forensics.

Biotech Research Support

This service provides various kinds of support for research in life sciences. Our lab-for-rent services, gives clients access to our state-of-the-art research lab with constant power, water and high speed Internet access to conduct their lab work under the supervision of our in-house technicians and scientists. Our ProjectDesigner provides deign of life sciences based projects with various milestones and cost analysis. We work with the researcher from project conception to full implementation; this is done with approval from the researcher's supervisor who is carried along throughout the project duration

Training and Workshops

We provide Genomics and Bioinformatics based training through our African Biosciences Research Institute division. We hold various workshops that teaches various aspect of Genomics and Bioinformatics Techniques. They usually last from 3 to 5 days. And we have a more advanced professional training that is fully residential and emphasizes in-depth practical techniques.

Institutional Support

We help research institutes both public and private to design solutions that meets their mandates or areas of research interest in Genomics and Bioinformatics. We provide on-site capacity building training and technical assistance in any area of Molecular Biology and Genomics based research and in Bioscience emerging technologies.

Reagents and Lab Supplies

We provide access to Molecular Biology reagents and supplies, we currently represent Promega, Bioline, Solis Biodyne, MP Biomedicals, Thermofischer, Merc, etc. We have automated system for accessing reagents and supplies and we deliver directly to your lab or institution. We also provide Molecular Biology laboratory equipment and represent some major equipment manufactures. We have experience with equipping labs and providing routine maintenance of equipments.
