About African Biosciences

Your Bioscience Research Partner In Africa

African Biosciences Ltd. is a pioneer and innovative life sciences company providing research reagents, supplies and technical support services to the academic, research, healthcare diagnostics and forensic testing sectors in Nigeria and across Africa (through its subsidiaries).

We are dedicated to transforming life sciences research by providing scientific and technical support to the academic and research community by enabling and catalyzing the ability to conduct bioscience research.

Our mission is to be the premier bioscience research service provider in Nigeria and across the African continent to improve bioscience research capacity by providing e-commerce access to reagents and supplies, research capabilities in genomics, proteomics, and bioinformatics, bioscience research advisory services, bioscience technical training, lab-for-rent, contract research services, and specialized medical testing and DNA forensics.
